# UI/UX Landing To Dashboard Spec

## Overview

Making the UX of going from landing page to Dashboard much better.

## Requirements

- Make the user experience and styling of the landing page as similar as possible to the dashboard.
- Do not make changes to the content or its positioning of it.
- Just change the theme of the landing page much similar to that of the dashboard.
- For example change the buttons, background, Title Style, the overall colours, text's font, etc as your changes.
- Do not limit yourself to the changes mentioned above, do changes where ever you deem necessary without changing the structure or content of the page.
